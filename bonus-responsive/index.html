PRESENTATION

Back in the day, grandmothers used to prepare a breakfast that we might call humble today,but was considered very rich at the time! Today the recipe is still unchanged,just break a very fresh egg into a glass, beat it with sugar, and add coffee or hot milk,for children, or Marsala wine or aniseed for adults. This is the best Italian dessert, the most popular and beloved of them all, and it has also birthed many other versions, even an egg-free Tiramisù! There is also a strowberry and Nutella tiramisù! Although hte origins of this famous dessert are unclear, because it is dispute between the region of Veneto, Fruili-Venezia Giulia,Piedmont, and Tuscany, it remains a cornerstone of Italian cuisine and it's widely prepared from North to South. Unfortnately, we weren't able to find out who invented it, but we can certenly teach you how to prepare your best tiramisù ever. So here's the recipe: try it and let us know what you think!
